Favourite Podcasts

Ed Sheeran

Theo's comedy world tour has recently landed him in England and he was able to interview world famous singer Ed Sheeran.

This discussion with the different backgrounds of louisiana American and Halifax English is quite refreshing. Though Theo joked that americans should consider returning, they both discuss differences in cultures from their personal experiences.

Sean Strickland

In Theo's interview with rising MMA favourite Sean Strickland, we get to see a different perspective into their lives.

There are many tangents and tales but the true beauty in Theo's podcasting style is the way he is able to make any guest feel comfortable to speak their own tale and gives them the space to breathe without forcing or looking for a scoop.
